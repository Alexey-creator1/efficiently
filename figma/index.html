About the school

Welcome to our martial arts dojo (or place of the way), we have dedicated our selves to the study of authentic martial arts for the last 22 years.

Unlike many martial arts, those practiced in our dojo require no advantage in strength or speed, and can be practised successfully by women, men and children of all ages and abilities. In addition to the new skills you will gain the also develop flexibility, coordination, concentration, improved health and fitness and self-esteem.
